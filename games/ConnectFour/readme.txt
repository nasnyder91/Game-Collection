<strong>Players</strong>: 2<br/><br/>

<strong>Rules</strong>: Players choose either black or red and who goes first.  Players take turns dropping their chips from the top of the board.
First player to get 4 of their colored chips in a row, horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, wins.
If no player was able to get 4 in a row and the board is full, the game is a draw.
Winner goes first next round.
